## ICP 1: Speech/Dictation Products

### Example: Nuance Dragon Medical
- **Domain**: nuance.com
- **Product**: Dragon Medical One - AI-powered clinical documentation
- **ICP**: 1
- **Reasoning**: Medical dictation tool, speech-to-text for healthcare
- **Blynt Fit**: High - needs real-time transcription with medical vocabulary boosting

### Example: Otter.ai (Dictation Mode)
- **Domain**: otter.ai
- **Product**: Voice meeting notes and transcription
- **ICP**: 1 (dictation) or 2 (meetings)
- **Reasoning**: Primary use case is speech-to-text input
- **Blynt Fit**: High - needs accurate real-time transcription
